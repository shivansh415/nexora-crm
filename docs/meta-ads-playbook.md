## Campaign Setup to Attract Buyers Who Can Afford You

1. **Objective:** Engagement → Messaging apps → WhatsApp (later: Leads objective with "maximise conversations")
2. **Filter for affordability in the copy, not just targeting** — the "₹2L+/month" line in Variant A makes broke leads self-disqualify before they click. Never remove the price-anchor sentence.
3. **Audience:**
   - Location: metro + tier-1 cities first (Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bangalore, Pune, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad, Indore)
   - Age 27–55
   - Detailed targeting: Business page admins, Real estate (industry), Small business owners, Facebook ads (behavior: engaged with business tools)
   - Exclude: job seekers interests
4. **Budget:** start ₹800–1,500/day per ad set, kill anything above ₹150/qualified conversation after 3 days
5. **The AI is your qualifier:** your existing prompt already asks business, requirement, budget, expectation. Leads that answer the budget question = genuine buyers. Track "budget answered" as your real KPI, not raw conversations.
6. **Retarget:** 7-day engagers who messaged but didn't book → run ad 3 (stat creative) at them.
